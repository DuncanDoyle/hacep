HACEP

HACEP is a Scalable and Highly Available architecture for Drools Complex Event Processing. HACEP combines Infinispan, Camel, ActiveMQ and obviously Drools in a unique way to obtain:

  • CEP Automatic horizontal scalability
  • CEP Automatic fail over
